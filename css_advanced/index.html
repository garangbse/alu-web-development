F.A.Q.

What programming languages do you teach?

We offer comprehensive courses in JavaScript, Python, React, Node.js, HTML/CSS, and many other modern programming languages and frameworks. Our curriculum is regularly updated to reflect industry standards.

Are the courses suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! Our courses are designed for all skill levels, from complete beginners to advanced developers. Each course includes step-by-step instructions and hands-on projects to ensure progressive learning.

How long does it take to complete a course?

Course duration varies depending on the topic and your learning pace. Most courses range from 2-8 hours of content, designed to be completed over several weeks with practice exercises and projects.

Do I get a certificate upon completion?

Yes! Upon successfully completing a course and passing the final assessment, you'll receive an industry-recognized certificate that you can add to your professional portfolio and LinkedIn profile.
